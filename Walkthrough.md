# Game Walkthrough & Visual Story Preview

### Explore the game's narrative through gameplay snippets and screenshots, demonstrating how the Ultron-Echo evolves from a harmless observer into an adaptive neuromorphic intelligence that learns from every player action.

---

The game begins with you trapped inside an abandoned S.H.I.E.L.D. laboratory. The Safe Room is the only place protected from the unknown entity lurking within the facility.
<img width="1918" height="780" alt="StartScreen" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a54312d6-0fee-4713-b153-b4d0dedf70ff" />

As you step outside the room, you encounter a small machine known as the Ultron-Echo. It appears harmless—little more than a forgotten remnant of Ultron's defeated consciousness.
<img width="1918" height="876" alt="Ultron-Echo" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/76f5641e-fdd0-4a3e-9d27-f6ce51f08670" />

With no immediate threat in sight, you begin exploring the laboratory. Ventilation tunnels, abandoned research chambers, hidden loot caches, and classified experiments reveal fragments of what happened here.
<img width="1918" height="790" alt="Environment" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0d2e560a-815e-4a01-aab4-293e883dacb1" />

As night falls, you return to the Safe Room to sleep. S.H.I.E.L.D. has promised extraction soon, and for now, the room feels secure.
<img width="1920" height="1080" alt="SleepScreen" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/83ed7ad5-b4cd-4c90-9843-8225ea7e3a9a" />

But while you sleep, something is watching.
Something is learning.
The Ultron-Echo silently studies your actions, recording every movement, every decision, and every habit.
<img width="1920" height="1080" alt="ItReadsYou" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a073ebb9-62fd-43d5-9e87-e1ed4a0ecd94" />

Day after day, the machine follows you. At first it merely observes. Then it adapts. The more you explore, hide, run, and survive, the more it understands who you are.
<img width="1920" height="1080" alt="Ultron-Learn" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dc02570c-eaf0-41cc-9bff-ce0c5cc2c284" />

Was S.H.I.E.L.D. simply too late to rescue you?
Or did you fail to notice the exact moment when the harmless machine became something far more dangerous?
